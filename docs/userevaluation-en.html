Abstract

The UserEvaluation Ontology is an ontology that models the assessments, made by a User of, for example, an Explanation. An assessment activity is performed to evaluate the subject (e.g. Explanation) using a metric (e.g. a Question) which is designed to evaluate some aspect of the subject, such as how understandable or useful it is. The outcome of the assessment is captured in the Result concept. The Prov properties startedAtTime and endedAtTime are used to record when the assessment took place. This pattern is based on that of Qual-O defined in C. Baillie, P. Edwards, and E. Pignotti, 2015. QUAL: A Provenance-Aware Quality Model. J. Data and Information Quality 5, 3, Article 12 (February 2015) DOI:https://doi.org/10.1145/2700413. This ontology was created as part of the iSee project (https://isee4xai.com) which received funding from EPSRC under the grant number EP/V061755/1. iSee is part of the CHIST-ERA pathfinder programme for European coordinated research on future and emerging information and communication technologies.
